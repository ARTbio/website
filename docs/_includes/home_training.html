Training

We, at the Freiburg Galaxy Team, are caring about training.

Twice a year, we are organizing a week of workshop on NGS data analyses here in Freiburg. Parallely, we are also regularly giving training around the world on data analyses and also on development and administration of Galaxy. You can check our events to find our next workshop.

Unfortunately, we can not fill all the demand in training. We offer training materials, accessible online at http://training.galaxyproject.org
